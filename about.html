About Us

About Us

NextRobotics Academy is a 4th IR technology solution provider. We are building a new generation of Nigerian Youth through technology, hands-on experience, real-life application and problem-solving programs that nourish the new-age skills. It is a tech academy that guides and educates children, teenagers, and adults(No age limit) on how to develop strong technical skills that will make them highly sought-after job candidates and enable them to work for themselves as independent contractors.
